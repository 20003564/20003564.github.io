Hongkongers have started different protests thus far, as there was a extradition bill proposed by the SAR government which aims to transfer the fugitives not only for Taiwan, but also for China.

My groupmates and I decided to have a quality production which aims to play on big screen with theatre setting, such as 4K screening, 5.1 surround sound base and with meaningful topic. So we record the current situation like a short documentary and try to use it to express our normal daily life have become unusual.
